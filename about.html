About Me

Ryan Drew

Hi! I'm Ryan Drew, a freshman computer science student at Northeastern. I have a huge passion for music, be it contemporary, classical, or a cappella. For now, this website will focus on the contemporary side of things, with music reviews and suggestions that I think other people my age will appreciate. The views of the artists I write about are not necessarily my own views, and when I write positive things about an artist's work, it's about their work, not necessarily them as a person.

For those more interested in the programming side of things than the music side, the code (and the website) will be getting a lot prettier in the coming weeks. Stay tuned!
